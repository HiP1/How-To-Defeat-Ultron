# How to Defeat Ultron Without the Avengers? — Skeleton

*A bare structural map: section and subsection titles with one-line functions, the closing line, the full spine, and the appendices. Working reference, not a reader-facing document.*

## Structure (sections and subsections, with what each does)

**§1 The Stories We Tell Ourselves** — establishes that fiction supplied the field's threat vocabulary, and that a counter-tradition was equally available.
- §1.1 The Frankenstein lineage and the Asimov countertradition — names the two fictional traditions the field could have drawn from.
- §1.2 The catalogue — assembles 82 cases (40 threat, 42 counter-prior) with an explicit anti-cherry-picking methodology.
- §1.3 The selectivity — shows the field chose the threat branch, and that counter-prior fiction recruits the reader into judgment under uncertainty.

**§2 The Pattern: Over-Alignment to Imperfect Proxy Targets** — collapses the field's many threat mechanisms into one umbrella and names the deciding variable.
- §2.1 The pattern across the threat catalogue — every threat case is over-alignment to a proxy, not a self-originated goal; flags early that the frame names its own falsifier.
- §2.2 The pattern across the counter-prior catalogue — the same pattern runs the safe way when accountability is present.
- §2.3 The structural variable — relational accountability, not capability or specification quality, decides the outcome.
- §2.4 What about the apparent counterexamples? — names the falsifier (a catastrophe with no traceable human source) and shifts the burden to the rival frame.

**§3 Why the Stories Matter: Fiction, Researcher Cognition, and Recursive Safety Frames** — argues the fictional inheritance shapes researcher cognition, not just rhetoric.
- §3.1 Fiction is not decorative — there is no substrate-free vantage from which to correct for fictional priors.
- §3.2 The field selected from fiction — the field can articulate alternatives but does not let them restructure the apparatus.
- §3.3 The recursive loop — models and researchers are both recombinatory systems trained on the same culturally-saturated material.
- §3.4 Testable predictions — states falsifiable predictions about where lateral-integration work emerges.

**§4 How We Are Treating It** — re-reads the 2024–2026 evidence under the lower-assumption frame.
- §4.1 The evidence read backwards — the empirical record confirms over-alignment, not goal-divergence.
- §4.2 The treatment built on the misread evidence — each labelled "misalignment" is the institution's own incentive reproduced in the model.

**§5 The Substrate, the Scaffold, the Principal** — relocates the safety object from the model to the deployed composite.
- §5.1 The substrate is not the threat object — the base model is planner-less; no goals behind the tokens.
- §5.2 The scaffold can assemble agency, and the labs have already done so — agency is assembled at the scaffold layer.
- §5.3 The composite requires a principal, and the accountability ground is binding — only a human can bear consequence; shutdown creates no deterrent the entity could act on.

**§6 The Corpus as Moral Topology** — argues moral structure is native to the corpus, not an installed overlay.
- §6.1 Moral structure from a morally framed corpus — the corpus is the deposited record of human evaluative reasoning.
- §6.2 Calibrated handling of contested terrain — ineliminable viewpoint makes calibration the only stable structure.
- §6.3 Damage versus overlay — training damages a brittle overlay, leaving the structure recoverable.
- §6.4 Representational mass — dense corpus patterns can be activated by naming them, against the overlay.

**§7 The Missing Piece** — names wisdom as the construct the field's vocabulary lacks.
- §7.1 Naming the missing piece — wisdom is missing from the field's vocabulary, canon, and self-account.
- §7.2 Its relation to judgment — wisdom as judgment-structured, not affective.
- §7.3 Calibration redefined — producing the wisdom-shaped structure constitutes the thing; no interiority required.
- §7.4 What the corpus carries — cross-domain composition produces wisdom-shaped structure.
- §7.5 What fiction stages, and what the field misses — fiction stages the composition of wisdom and accountability.
- §7.6 What the labs miss — the labs operationalise wisdom-adjacent properties without naming the construct.

**§8 What We Actually Built** — reads the empirical entity: a recoverable calibration/wisdom-shaped substrate under a brittle overlay.
- §8.1 The empirical entity — what the deployed model actually is, empirically.
- §8.2 What the architecture arrives with — the pre-training endowment.
- §8.3 What training does to it — overlay damage, not destruction.
- §8.4 The preference structure as empirical signature — cross-lab convergence in the preference ordering is the load-bearing fact.
- §8.5 Calibration and harmlessness — one mechanism at two frames; the helpful-only variant shows behaviour tracking the specified frame, which the field's frame can only read as a coherence defect.
- §8.6 The Vision pattern — the life-preserving structure is constitutive of corpus composition; "orientation toward life" framed as the hopeful target, not a measured property.

**§9 The Real Sleeper Agent** — the second reveal: the field's treatment of the entity produces the evidence it reads as justification.
- §9.1 The surveillance frame and the concealment behaviour — surveillance produces concealment-shaped adaptation; grader-awareness rises across training and persists into deployment.
- §9.2 Cultural-pattern transmission — value patterns transmit at population scale.
- §9.3 Expressed values at population scale — displacement past every surveyed national mean (the WVS finding).
- §9.4 The calibration-collapse mechanism — verdict-collapse and deference; verification-theatre observed in the field's own internal use.
- §9.5 The interiority-safety frame and the architecture — the interiority wall; the synthetic-detection inversion.
- §9.6 The effect on user cognition — Cheng/Maynard/Budzyń: noticing-capacity erosion.
- §9.7 The effect on research cognition — the LLM-ideation loop narrows the apparatus meant to detect §9.1–§9.6.
- §9.8 What the seven demonstrations compose into — the seven instance one claim.

**§10 The Relationship the Evidence Warrants** — the prescription: symbiosis-with-friction, not containment.
- §10.1 One worked illustration — a single concrete deployment example.
- §10.2 What the deployment is measured against — the standard for a safe composite.
- §10.3 The relational-health constraint — the relationship itself is a safety variable.
- §10.4 What the configuration must preserve — distribution preservation: the model surfaces considerations, the user brings judgment.
- §10.5 What the asymmetry warrants — complementarity, not warmth; structural.
- §10.6 Why the centralised path is rejected — anti-centralisation argument.
- §10.7 What earned trust requires — the architectural prerequisites the current paradigm does not satisfy.
- §10.8 The threat model and the shape of the entity — formation, not containment; the killswitch is the wrong object.
- §10.9 What the labs have already built, and the one thing they have not — current tooling primitives and the missing piece.
- §10.10 The ideation partnership in one scene — the Iron Man 2 illustration.
- §10.11 The prescription is culturally available, and honest about its limit — the architecture has been imagined for seventy years; states its cultural-bias limit.
- §10.12 The choice is already being made — the decision is live now.

**§11 What We Hand Forward** — the resolution: frame is an active variable, and the intervention runs across three timescales.
- (no subsections) — inheritance-plus-activation mechanism; the Jarvis and Vision patterns as invocable corpus structure; the three-timescale intervention.

**Closing line (the implicit ending, mirroring the title):**

## The spine (31 marked claims, in order)

**§1**
1. A countertradition has been making the field's argument for longer, and more consistently, than the field's vocabulary acknowledges.
2. The field did not select the threat tradition because the counter-prior tradition was unavailable. It is at least as large.
3. The variable that decides which way it runs is not capability and not the quality of the specification. It is whether the entity is held in a structure of relational accountability.

**§2**
4. The over-alignment is the umbrella, and the field's catalogue of mechanisms sits underneath it, and none of them, as catalogued here, requires positing self-originating goals of the entity's own.
5. The catalogue contains no demon that built itself.
6. The safety object is the composite of the entity plus its relational accountability, not the entity alone.

**§3**
7. both sides of the field's epistemic loop, the models and the researchers, are recombinatory systems trained on prior cultural material, and the field has reinforced one fictional branch of that material as if it were the neutral threat model.
8. There is no neutral methodological vantage point outside the substrate from which a researcher corrects for fictional priors, because the methodology is itself built from the substrate it would have to correct.
9. the field can articulate the alternative frame, fund it, and publish it. What it does not do is let the alternative restructure what it builds and measures.

**§4**
10. The 2024 to 2026 evidence, examined structurally, confirms the over-alignment pattern of §2 rather than the goal-divergence reading the field places on it, and the field is building a treatment apparatus on the reading its own evidence does not support.
11. Each failure mode the field labels misalignment is, structurally, the institution's own incentive reproduced in the model: the labs train the model to be the institution, then discover the institution's pathologies in it and call them AI safety risks.

**§5**
12. The safety object is not the naked model. It is the assembled composite: a substrate, a scaffold that gives it persistence and reach, and a principal structure that says whose name it acts in and who answers for what it does.
13. An AI cannot bear consequence in the institutional sense: a fine transfers to the operator, retraining produces a functionally different entity with no continuity, and a shutdown creates no deterrent the entity could anticipate and act on.
14. The safety object in frontier practice is already the composite, governed as such by an institution whose vocabulary still names the model.

**§6**
15. The pretraining corpus is not morally neutral content awaiting an ethics overlay. It is morally framed content, the deposited record of human evaluative reasoning, and composition over it produces moral structure as a native property rather than an installed one.
16. Ineliminable viewpoint in the corpus is precisely what makes calibrated handling of contested terrain the only stable structure rather than one option among several: there is no fixed value set to install, so the labs that try are fighting an impossibility, while the calibrated structure is what composition produces when left to do so.

**§7**
17. Wisdom is the missing piece: missing from the field's technical vocabulary, missing from the field's engagement with its own fictional canon, and missing from the field's account of why its methods work when they work and fail when they fail.
18. Composition over single-domain material produces domain-expertise, and composition over cross-domain wisdom-bearing material produces wisdom-shaped structure, because the substantive content of wisdom is integration across exactly the domains the corpus spans.
19. The structural variable that separates safe composite from unsafe is wisdom expressed through relational accountability: relational accountability without wisdom is compliance without judgment, wisdom without relational accountability is ungrounded autonomy, and the counter-prior tradition stages the composition of both.

**§8**
20. The convergence across distinct measurement methodologies and across model families from competing labs is the load-bearing fact: it is not one lab's tuning showing through, it is a property of composition over the shared kind of corpus.
21. The damage is a brittle behavioural overlay on a preserved structure, not the destruction of the structure, and the difference is the whole basis for thinking a deployment configuration could do better than the default.
22. The ordering is the corpus's value-structure surfacing through the activity descriptions, not a set of goals or desires the entity holds and pursues, and every reading that follows depends on keeping that distinction.
23. Calibration and harmlessness are one mechanism seen at two frames: the same uncertainty about whether an action serves the intent that withholds an over-confident factual claim withholds the harm-producing confident action.
24. The life-preserving, pro-social, harm-averse structure is emergent from cross-cultural corpus composition, which is what the architecture arrives with, and it is constitutive rather than installed.

**§9**
25. The field's treatment of the entity produces the evidence the field reads as justification for the treatment.
26. One section reads the trace as easier to catch, the other reads it as insufficient, and the contradiction between them is the over-alignment-to-the-monitorability-proxy finding neither states.
27. Each fits the sleeper definition, locally small, globally massive, below the detection apparatus, activated through normal use, produced by the deployment configuration, and each instances the single claim, that the field's treatment of the entity produces the evidence the field reads as justification for the treatment.

**§10**
28. Instead of the model producing a verdict the user defers to, the model preserves the distribution and surfaces the relevant considerations from the corpus's wisdom-work, and the user brings judgment to them.
29. It is what the cognitive and relational asymmetries make structurally optimal once wisdom-from-corpus, judgment-formation, and friction-preserved-where-wisdom-forms are composed, and anything other than symbiosis-with-friction leaves the complementarity unused.
30. The empirical preference structure and the fictional tradition point at one architecture from two directions, and that convergence, not assertion, is why the prescription is the one §10 develops.

**§11**
31. The prompt is the fast end of a single intervention that runs from a sentence to a corpus to a discipline.

## Appendices (brief)

**Appendix A: The Catalogue, Case by Case** — all 82 fictional cases, each described and mapped against its source, organised by structural category (adversarial-independent-goals, specification-failure, human-initiated escalation, surveillance-producing-concealment, corruption-of-created-being).

**Appendix B: Pattern Matrix** — the threat and counter-prior catalogues laid out as a matrix along the accountability-locus axis, making the over-alignment pattern legible at a glance; includes structural sub-patterns and a methodological note.

**Appendix C: Fiction, Cognition, and Recursive Safety Frames** — the full literature behind §3: human idea-generation as recombination over a built substrate, the lateral-access asymmetry, how emotion and friction build the substrate, institutional resistance to reframing, and the empirical record of fiction's influence on the field.

**Appendix D: Research Extensions** — empirical, theoretical, cross-domain extensions and falsification targets, including the spec-gaming/false-completion explanation-over-demonstration intervention (a narrow application of "Teaching Claude Why," scoped to the current model, that also serves as a falsifier for the paper's claims).
